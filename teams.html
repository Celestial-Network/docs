Teams

Teams are an essential part of the Celestial Network YAML. I will show you a step by step guide to defining teams with YAML


1) Below the map info section define your teams.

teams:

2) Indent the next section and define your first team.

teams:
  red:

3) Indent in again and define some of your teams characteristics. You can also define some gamemode objectives here such as where the wool should be placed in Capture The Wool.

teams:
  red:
    title: 'Evil'
    spawn: 50,25,5,90,0
    max: 25

title: 'Evil' defines the title of the team and is the team name that will be shown in the chat.

spawn: 50,25,5,90,0 tells the plugin where the team will spawn. It follows the format X,Y,Z,Yaw,Pitch.

max: 25 tells the plugin that the maximum number of players for that team is 25.

4) Now do the same for the other team

teams:
  red:
    title: 'Evil'
    spawn: 50,25,5,90,0
    max: 25
  yellow:
    title: 'Happy'
    spawn: 56,25,4,90,0
    max: 25
