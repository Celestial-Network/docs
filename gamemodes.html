Gamemodes

For each of the following gamemodes it is essential that you set the value of type: to the value in the code box.

Destroy The Monument dtm

The objective of Destroy The Monument is to destroy the opposing team's block (commonly obsidian). This may be reffered to as the objective and multiple objectives may be set per map.

Monuments must be set and defined under each teams configuration section. The team must destroy the monuments listed under the team config.

Note: A maximum of 2 teams may be set for a Destroy The Monument map

Below shows an example team configuration a dtm map

teams:
  lime:
    spawn: 30.5,48,-854.5,0,0
    title: Lime
    monuments: # these monuments must be destroyed by lime
      a: # the id of the monument must be unique from one another
        name: Flag Tower
        location: 20,70,-569 # the coordinate of the obsidian block
      b:
        name: Watch Tower
        location: 36,65,-561
      c:
        name: Bell Tower
        location: 43,57,-582
  red:
    spawn: 33.5,48,-561.5,180,0
    title: Red
    monuments: # these monuments must be destroyed by red
      a:
        name: Flag Tower
        location: 42,70,-849
      b:
        name: Watch Tower
        location: 26,65,-857
      c:
        name: Bell Tower
        location: 19,57,-836

Capture The Wool ctw

Capture The Wool has the objective of returning a block of wool which is commonly collected in a wool room on the opposing teams side, and place it in a monument box also known as the Victroy Monument.

Wools must be set and defined under each teams configuration section. The team must must place the wools in the correct location specified listed under the team config.

Below shows an example team configuration a capture the wool map

teams:
  red:
    spawn: 292.5,25,-55.5,90,0
    title: Red
    max: 25
    wools:
      orange:
        name: Orange Wool
        chest: -5,37,4
        monument: 292,25,-62
      pink:
        name: Pink Wool
        chest: -49,34,-56
        monument: 298,25,-56
      green:
        name: Green Wool
        chest: -5,37,-116
        monument: 292,25,-50
  yellow:
    spawn: 24.5,25,-55.5,-90,0
    title: Yellow
    max: 25
    wools:
      cyan:
        name: Cyan Wool
        chest: 321,37,-116
        monument: 24,25,-50
      purple:
        name: Purple Wool
        chest: 365,34,-56
        monument: 18,25,-56
      lime:
        name: Lime Wool
        chest: 321,37,4
        monument: 24,25,-62

Team Death Match tdm

In Team Death Match a maximum of 4 teams compete with each other each with the aim of getting the highest amount of kills in 10 minutes. Each kill will score a point and the teams total points is shown on the scoreboard.

Team Death Match requires no additional parts to the YAML
